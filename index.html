About the School

New Delhi Public School (NDPS), Buxar is a well-known private school in Bihar that provides quality education based on the CBSE curriculum. The school focuses on building a strong academic foundation while also encouraging students to grow in creativity, discipline, and confidence. Established around 2010–2011, NDPS aims to deliver modern education using smart teaching methods and experienced teachers. The school offers classes from Playgroup to Class 10 and follows both English and Hindi as mediums of instruction.

School Facilities

New Delhi Public School, Buxar is equipped with modern infrastructure and amenities to provide students with a comprehensive learning environment. Our facilities are designed to support academic excellence, physical development, and overall personality growth.

🖥️

Smart Classrooms

Technology-enabled classrooms with digital boards and multimedia resources for interactive learning experiences.

💻

Computer Lab

Well-equipped computer laboratory with modern systems and internet connectivity for digital literacy training.

🔬

Science Laboratory

Fully functional science lab with equipment for physics, chemistry, and biology practical experiments.

📚

Library

Extensive collection of books, magazines, and educational resources to encourage reading habits and research skills.

⚽

Sports Ground

Spacious playground with facilities for cricket, football, basketball, and other outdoor sports activities.
